  • Computer Science Teaching Assistant - Department of Computer Science | Colgate University

    At Colgate University's Department of Computer Science, I've had the privilege of serving as a Teaching Assistant since the Fall of 2022. My primary responsibilities have included tutoring 20 to 30 students each semester. I've passionately guided these students in debugging their code, enabling them to foster enhanced proficiency and confidence in their computer programming journey. Notably, the curriculum delved into advanced algorithms such as BFS, DFS, and dynamic programming.

    Additionally, I conducted weekly open-lab sessions. These sessions were critical in providing hands-on experience, focusing on the optimization of Java and Python code. We delved deeply into the nuances of Object-Oriented programming, dissected complex data structures, and navigated the intricacies of run-time complexities. This role has not only allowed me to impart knowledge but also reaffirmed my own understanding of these foundational concepts.
